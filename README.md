## About Me

Data analyst specialising in **climate and sustainability**, with experience turning messy, complex datasets into clear decisions for non-technical stakeholders.

Currently completing a **Data Science MSc** while working as a freelance data analyst in the climate and non-profit sector. My projects focus on real-world environmental impact; from air pollution exposure around schools to forecasting household carbon savings from flexible electricity use.

Background in education sharpened my ability to communicate technical findings to non-technical audiences, something I bring to every dashboard and report I build.

- Focused on **climate, sustainability, and social impact data**
- Experienced in **Tableau, SQL, Python, and machine learning**
- Open to **permanent roles in the UK & Spain**
- Always learning: currently deepening skills in **big data and cloud tools**

## Featured Projects

### 🏫 School-centric Air Pollution in England
> Analysed 24,000+ schools using PostGIS to prioritise £50M in pollution interventions — identifying 10x higher ROI priorities vs alternatives. Built an interactive Tableau dashboard with budget simulator for educational policymakers, protecting children in high-exposure areas 3–4x more efficiently than even distribution.

[![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Repo-uk--school--air--pollution-2D6A4F?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/KrisN13/uk-school-air-pollution)

### ⚡ Forecasting UK Grid Carbon Intensity & Household CO₂ Impact
> Modelled household CO₂ reductions of up to 30% by forecasting UK grid carbon intensity and building appliance usage scenarios against predictive grid data (2020–2025). Deployed as an interactive Streamlit app allowing users to calculate their own potential savings.

[![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Repo-Forecasting--UK--Grid--Carbon--Intensity-2D6A4F?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/KrisN13/Forecasting-UK-Grid-Carbon-Intensity)
